Chicken Tortilla Soup

An authentic Mexican soup; the traditional soup has chicken, fried tortilla strips, garlic, onion, chiles and tomatoes.There are other varietes that include beans, sour cream and cheese.

Preparing and Serving

Averaging about 40 minutes to make, chicken tortilla soup is a great and simple soup to make anytime. You can saute onions and garlic, then add chili powder, oregano, tomatoes, broth and water. Allow to simmmer, then you can hominy, chiles, beans, chicken and cilantro. Let it simmer for a few more minutes then serve with your favorite garnishes. The soup can be erved with salad or by itself with avocados, sour cream and cheese.

Storage

To store it, let it cool down completely and ladle it to airtight containers. Storein the fridge for up to four days. Reheat in the microwave or stove. You can also freeze the soup. Same process as with a refrigerator. Freezing the soup with make it last up to three months.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1(28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1(10.5 ounce) can condensed chicken broth
  • 1 1/4 cups of water
  • 2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, rinsed and drained
  • 2 large boneless chicken breast halves, cooked and cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup white hominy
  • 1 (4 ounce) can chopped green chile peppers
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1/2 cup crushed tortilla chips, or to taste
  • 2 medium avocados, sliced, or to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded Monterey Jack cheese, or to taste
  • 2 tablespoons chopped green onions, or to taste
Steps
  1. Heat oil in a stockpot over medium heat. Add onion and garlic; saute until soft, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, condensed broth, water, chili powder, and oregano; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 to 10 minutes.
  2. Stir in black beans, cooked chicken, corn, hominy, chile peppers, and cilantro. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  3. Ladle soup into individual serving bowls, and top with crushed tortilla chips, avocado slices, Monterey Jack cheese, and green onions.
